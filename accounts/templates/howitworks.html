Frequently Asked Questions

How much does it cost to hire through WorkConnect?

Posting projects and reviewing freelancer proposals is completely free. You only pay when you hire a freelancer, with our platform fee being just 3% of the project cost.

How do I ensure I'm hiring the right freelancer?

All freelancers on WorkConnect are vetted through our verification process. You can review their portfolios, client feedback, and even conduct interviews before hiring.

What payment methods are supported?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Our secure payment system holds funds in escrow until work is completed to your satisfaction.

Freelancer FAQs

How do I get paid for my work?

WorkConnect offers multiple payment options including direct bank transfer, PayPal, and Payoneer. Payments are released from escrow when clients approve your work.

What fees does WorkConnect charge?

We charge a 10% service fee on all completed projects. There are no membership fees or hidden charges.

How can I increase my chances of getting hired?

Complete your profile with a professional photo, detailed skills, and portfolio samples. Write personalized proposals and start with competitive rates to build your reputation.
